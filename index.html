About TickCounter Countdown

TickCounter lets you make a customized countdown timer to any date in just a few clicks.

A countdown timer is a tool that shows how much time left to a specified date - how many days, hours, minutes and seconds remain to Black Friday, Halloween or any other event.

Trusted by 1.9+ million websites, our countdowns work with any platform - WordPress, Shopify, Squarespace and all the others. No code skills required.

Need help with anything? Contact us!

How do I make a countdown timer?

  1. Select the date and time for your countdown.
  2. Tweak the options above until you're happy with the preview.
  3. Click "Make Countdown".
  4. That's it, now share your countdown or embed it on your website.
